Before you write

Most issues fall into one of these. Try these first.

I'm not getting notifications

  • Open iPhone Settings → Notifications → ShiftSleeper. Turn alerts and sounds on.
  • In ShiftSleeper → Settings → Integrations → "Request again" to re-prompt.
  • Focus Modes (Sleep, Do Not Disturb) can silence alerts. Add ShiftSleeper as an Allowed App.

HealthKit isn't syncing

  • Open iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → ShiftSleeper. Enable all categories.
  • Force-quit and reopen ShiftSleeper.
  • HealthKit only shows sleep recorded by Apple Watch or third-party apps. If your data lives in Oura, Whoop, etc., enable their HealthKit write permission.

My widget shows old data

  • Open the main app once. The app publishes a fresh snapshot when it launches or when you change a shift.
  • Long-press the widget → Edit Widget → toggle the option and back. Forces a reload.

My Apple Watch doesn't show the app

  • Open the Watch app on your iPhone → My Watch → scroll to ShiftSleeper → "Show App on Apple Watch" must be on.
  • Install can take a minute or two over Bluetooth.

I want to cancel a subscription

  • iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → ShiftSleeper → Cancel.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
  • For refunds: reportaproblem.apple.com.

I want to delete all my data

  • iPhone Settings → General → iPhone Storage → ShiftSleeper → Delete App.
  • Everything is on-device. There is no cloud copy to delete.
